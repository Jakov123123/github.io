About Us

Boatify is a premier boat rental platform connecting boat owners with sailing enthusiasts across the beautiful Croatian coast.

Founded in 2018 by a team of passionate sailors, we've grown to become the most trusted name in Croatian maritime tourism, offering over 500 boats across 9 major marinas.

Our mission is simple: make boating accessible to everyone while ensuring the highest standards of safety, comfort, and customer service.
